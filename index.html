Practium with QuadReal

The sponsor for this practicum is QuadReal Property Group, which is a commercial real estate (CRE) investment, development, and operating company. The practicum will be centered on a single overlying objective which is to identify suitable CRE assets for acquisition based on a series of spatial, non-spatial, leading, and non-leading indicators that the affect opportunity and risk of a potential CRE asset. The main objectives of this practicum will be to obtain working experience in applying spatial analytics to commercial real estate, to conduct site suitability analysis based on different indicators, as well as utilizing H3 spatial indexing, and Python spatial libraries for both analyzing and visualizing results. The deliverables consist of a web map visualizing the suitability of CRE assets based on a multi-criteria decision analysis, a directory of scripts used in the analysis and a final report of the entire project detailing project background, data sources, methodology, results, and conclusion. Datasets used include places, parcel, zoning, transportation and infrastructure datasets. This analysis will leverage open-source tools including Python, Folium map package, Leaflet, PostgreSQL, and QGIS to complete the analysis. A general methodology for this practicum, involves first establishing and quantifying the criteria to determine the suitability of a site based on a series of indicators, obtaining the necessary datasets, data cleaning and preprocessing, site suitability analysis, data visualization and finally project documentation. The total hours estimated to complete the entire practicum amounts to 360 hours.
